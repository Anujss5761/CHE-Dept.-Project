About IIChE MMMUT

Chemical engineering is a fascinating field that touches almost every aspect of our lives! It's all about the design, development, and improvement of processes that transform raw materials into valuable products. Think of everything from the gasoline in your car to the medicine in your cabinet, the clothes you wear, and even the food you eat – all have likely been touched by the hands of a chemical engineer. Chemical engineers translate processes developed in the lab into practical applications for the commercial production of products, and then work to maintain and improve those processes. They rely on the main foundations of engineering: math, physics, and chemistry. Biology also plays an increasingly important role.


About IIChE

IIChE stands for the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers, the apex professional body for chemical engineers in India. It was founded on the eve of Indian independence in 1947 with the aim of promoting chemical engineering education and the profession in general.

IIChE's mission is to:

> Advance the science and practice of chemical engineering

> Promote the professional development of its members

> Serve as a national resource for the chemical engineering industry

> Foster international collaboration with other chemical engineering organizations

IIChE has a membership of over 15,000, including:

> Advance the science and practice of chemical engineering

> Promote the professional development of its members

> Serve as a national resource for the chemical engineering industry

> Foster international collaboration with other chemical engineering organizations

The institute has its headquarters in Kolkata and has 42 regional centers and 172 student chapters spread throughout India.

Vision
To promote advancement of Chemical Engineering Science and draw up a code of ethics in the profession. To maintain and widen contacts with chemical engineering professionals in India and abroad ensure regular exchange of ideas with other national and international professional institutes in this field.
Reach
IIChE is also a member of the Asia Pacific Confederation of Chemical Engineering, Australia and a corresponding member of the European Federation of Chemical Engineering, Germany.
Collaborations
IIChE maintains close association with various professional bodies at home and abroad. It has regular collaborations with two of the leading global Institutes in the field of Chemical Engineering, namely, the Institution of Chemical Engineers, UK and the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), USA.
Engineering
At home, in 2002, IIChE joined Engineering Council of India (ECI) as a member of its Board of Governors. IIChE is working with ECI for enlisting chemical engineers in National Registers for Professional Engineers.
